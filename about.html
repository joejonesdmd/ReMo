What is Re:Mo Vision?

Re:Mo Vision is the next step in Medical and AI Technology. By using State of the art facial recognition and AR software, We have been able to help people with a range of memory problems such as dementia.

By using heads up display technology, We are able to create lenses that show information about the people surrounding you such as family and close friends. The idea is to use preloaded information to recognise the targets face and display the information about them so that if the wearer would be estranged.

If Re:Mo Vision is introduced at an earlier stage it could potentially reduce the number of intense episodes.
